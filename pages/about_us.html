About Us

Greenfuel was founded by Everett Sistrunk to help homeowners get more information on green energy solutions. Greenfuel is a website full of compiled information from different sources, such as the United States Department of Energy, Wikipedia, among others. The intention is to be as user-friendly as possible to help you get the right information, such as basic information on three green energy sources for your home, cost, tax rebate information, and clean/green energy practices.
