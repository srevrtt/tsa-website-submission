FAQ

What's the point?

I made Greenfuel to compile information from a variety of sources about green energy for homeowners in a nice, clean format. Most of the time, if you want information about green energy solutions for your home, you need to go to many unorganized websites to get the information you need.

Do I need to sign up?

There is no sign up required.

Is this free?

Yes, absolutely! I believe information like this must be given away for free and without any pesky ads in the way of the information. I do not make any money from this and I have no plans to do so either.
